Hi there recruiter, colleague or curious internet adventurer! My name is Katie.

I'm a highly-achieving student who is passionate about computing and its potential to create positive change for businesses, individuals and wider society. I'm constantly seeking opportunities to learn and grow in this dynamic field - if I do not currently have the skills you're looking for, I'll make sure that's fixed before very long! I take it as a personal challenge.

In my spare time, I'm modifying videogames I like to play (Minecraft, Baldur's Gate, The Sims Series), I'm jotting down my thoughts on the ethical use-cases for AI, or I'm cracking open my keyboard to desolder its switches and lube its stabilizers. You might also catch me sinking far too many hours into FPS games (Overwatch 2, VALORANT, CS:GO).

I especially love to attempt to debug AI-hallucinated code, and I love to boot custom firmware onto gaming consoles (NSW, Wii, NDS, DSi, 3DS).

I spent the majority of my teenage years building and installing PC hardware units, locating and reverse-engineering exploits in Nintendo and Microsoft hardware, and creating vector-based graphic designs on an independently-contracted basis for various clients, both individuals and businesses.

If you're looking for a candidate who has a deep love for professional growth, a deep personal love for what they do, can perform under pressure and adapt to change quickly, think of me - Think KT.
